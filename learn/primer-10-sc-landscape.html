South Carolina has assembled an unusual density of AI-relevant institutions, deployments, and policy work for a state its size. The picture is scattered across regions, sectors, and audiences — which is part of why SCAIO exists.

The five regions

Where AI work happens in South Carolina.

Greenville / Upstate

BMW · Michelin · CU-ICAR

Manufacturing concentration. BMW Spartanburg's Figure AI pilot. Clemson's CU-ICAR applied research. Upstate startup activity.

Clemson

School of Computing · CU-ICAR · CMUSC Hub

Clemson's academic core for AI and computing. CU-ICAR. Partnership with MUSC on health AI.

Columbia / Midlands

State government · USC AI Institute

SC General Assembly, state agencies, AI Center of Excellence, and USC's AI Institute. Center of gravity for SC AI policy.

Charleston / Lowcountry

MUSC · NIWC Atlantic · SCRA · Boeing · Volvo

Clinical AI at MUSC. Federal cyber anchor (NIWC Atlantic). SCRA HQ. Aerospace and automotive manufacturing.

Statewide

SCRA · ADAPT · Tech College System

Programs and institutions that operate across regions — SCRA's AI Leadership Hub, ADAPT in SC EPSCoR, Technical College System.

The institutional anchors

Four institutions that carry most of SC's AI capacity.

Academic

USC AI Institute

SC's largest university-wide AI research institute. Interdisciplinary research, workforce development, commercialization. Based in Columbia.

Academic

Clemson School of Computing

Clemson's primary AI/computing research home. CU-ICAR adjacent. Strong applied-research portfolio.

Clinical

MUSC Center for AI + Clemson-MUSC AI Hub

SC's largest applied clinical-AI environment. DAX Copilot, Pap-smear AI, NCI-designated Hollings Cancer Center.

Convening

SCRA AI Leadership Hub

SCRA convening of public-private-academic AI leadership. Produced the SC AI Symposium and statewide AI report.

The policy picture

What the 126th General Assembly has on the table.

  • H.5253 · AI in Education — Parental consent and district governance for classroom AI tools.
  • S.443 · Health Claims and AI — Constraints on AI use in insurance prior-authorization decisions.
  • S.963 · AI Consumer Protection — General consumer protection in commercial AI applications.
  • H.5138 · Chatbot Protection Act — Disclosure and safeguards in conversational AI deployments.
  • S.1037 · Protecting Children from Chatbots Act — Specific protections for minors interacting with AI systems.
  • H.5085 · AI Week resolution — Establishing an annual AI literacy week.
  • S.225 · Open-source decentralized AI resolution — Statement of policy interest in open-source AI.
The major applied deployments

Where AI is already in production in South Carolina.

  • BMW Spartanburg + Figure AI — Humanoid robotics pilot at BMW's largest plant globally.
  • MUSC clinical AI — DAX Copilot for ambient documentation, AI radiology and pathology, Pap-smear AI.
  • SC state government "Bradley" assistant — Internal AI assistant under the AI Center of Excellence.
  • Mercedes-Benz Vans · Volvo · Michelin — Computer-vision quality control and predictive-maintenance in production.
  • Port of Charleston · SC Ports Authority — Ongoing digitization of port operations, including AI-augmented logistics.

South Carolina's AI ecosystem is scattered across university labs, state agencies, corporate boardrooms, and early-stage startups, with no central hub aggregating the landscape. SCAIO closes that information gap."

The infrastructure conversation

SC's data-center decade.

South Carolina is making infrastructure decisions about AI build-out — energy pricing, siting, water use, the V.C. Summer nuclear question — that will harden into precedent within five years.

Santee Cooper's large-load rate pilot is among the most thoughtful state-level approaches in the country. Meta's Orangeburg facility, the proposed Moc-1 Upstate project, and the V.C. Summer revival question are all live. SCAIO's flagship report Chapter 5 and the Data-Center Decade Journal piece treat this in depth.
